This talk was presented at the final round of <i>NYU PhD Live!</i> on March 31, 2026, at the <b>African Grove Theatre</b> at New York University. I was selected as a <b>Top 8 finalist</b>, and I was the <b>only first-year Ph.D. student</b> among the finalists.

PhD Live! is a university-wide public speaking showcase in which doctoral students present their research and ideas to a broad audience in a clear, engaging, and accessible way. This experience was especially meaningful to me because it allowed me to communicate my work beyond a technical research setting and connect with a wider academic community.
